Tic-Tac-Toe

tic-tac-toe is a simple game where two players (in this case, you and the computer) draw Xs and Os on a 3x3 grid, and the first player to get three of their sign in one row (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) wins. Click/Tap on a place in the grid to start the game!
